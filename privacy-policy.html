DFEAL uses information collected through AuditMD exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Engagement Management: Scheduling discovery calls, delivering assessment reports and deliverables, and managing the client lifecycle from intake through post-audit follow-up.

04 What We Never Do With Your Data

We believe the clearest way to earn trust is to be explicit about what we will never do. The following practices are prohibited within DFEAL LLC and are not subject to exception:

  • We do not sell your data. Client organisational data is never sold, licensed, rented, or traded to any third party for any consideration.
  • We do not share data with advertisers. No client data is disclosed to advertising platforms, data brokers, or marketing analytics providers.
  • We do not use client data for competitive intelligence. Assessment findings are used solely to serve the client who provided them — not to build benchmarks, market reports, or competitive analysis products.
  • We do not share deliverables as Drive links. All client deliverables (reports, scorecards, roadmaps) are delivered as encrypted attachments — never as public or shared Drive links.
  • We do not collect patient health information. AuditMD assesses organisational Health IT posture — not individual patient records. No Protected Health Information (PHI) or individually identifiable health information is collected through any AuditMD form or process.

05 Data Handling and Security Controls

DFEAL applies a layered security architecture to all client data, consistent with the HIPAA Security Rule standards we help our clients implement.

5.1 Transmission Security

All data submitted through auditmd.xyz is transmitted over TLS 1.2 or higher. Our website forms route through a server-side proxy (Netlify serverless function) — webhook URLs and API keys are never exposed to the browser or included in client-side code.

5.2 Storage and Access Control

Client data is stored in Google Workspace (Google Drive and Google Sheets) under the support@auditmd.xyz Google Workspace account. Access controls are role-based: only DFEAL staff with a direct engagement role have access to client-specific folders. External sharing of client folders is disabled.

5.3 Workflow Automation Security

Our engagement automation platform (Zapier) processes form submission data to route it to the correct internal workflow. DFEAL uses Zapier's premium business plan with Business Associate Agreement coverage. Zapier task logs are reviewed monthly and purged on a quarterly schedule.

5.4 Credential and Key Management

API keys, webhook URLs, and credentials used in our platform are stored exclusively as environment variables in Netlify's server-side environment. They are never hardcoded in client-side source code, committed to version control repositories, or transmitted in browser-accessible network requests.

5.5 Multi-Factor Authentication

Multi-factor authentication is enforced on all DFEAL accounts with access to client data, including support@auditmd.xyz (Google Workspace), Zapier, Netlify, and our project management tools.

5.6 Incident Response

In the event of a confirmed data security incident affecting client organisational data, DFEAL will notify affected clients within 72 hours of discovery, provide a written incident summary, and implement remediation measures. Incidents involving potential PHI exposure (if any were inadvertently collected) will be handled per HIPAA Breach Notification Rule requirements.

07 Data Retention

DFEAL retains client data only as long as necessary for the engagement and applicable legal obligations.

  • Active Engagement Data (forms, assessments, correspondence) — retained for the duration of the engagement plus 12 months post-completion to support any follow-up questions or dispute resolution.
  • Deliverables and Reports — retained for 36 months post-delivery. Clients are encouraged to maintain their own copies of all deliverables.
  • Financial Records (invoices, payment records) — retained for 7 years per standard U.S. business record-keeping requirements.
  • Website Log Data — Netlify server logs are retained per Netlify's standard retention schedule (approximately 30 days). DFEAL does not request or retain extended log archives.
  • Zapier Task Logs — reviewed monthly and purged quarterly. No task history is retained beyond 90 days.

Upon written request, DFEAL will confirm what data is held for your organization and provide a deletion timeline where retention obligations permit.

09 A Note on HIPAA and Patient Data

AuditMD is a Health IT compliance consulting service. We assess the organisational systems, policies, and controls that protect patient data — we do not access, collect, process, or review individual patient records or Protected Health Information (PHI) as part of any engagement.

No patient PHI is ever submitted to, processed by, or stored within the AuditMD platform. All AuditMD assessment forms explicitly collect organisational and operational data only.

Where DFEAL LLC acts as a Business Associate under HIPAA (in the event client engagement requires handling any information that could be considered PHI), a Business Associate Agreement will be executed prior to that data exchange. Our standard engagement scope does not require BA status.

Clients are responsible for ensuring that any information they voluntarily submit through our forms does not include individual patient identifiers, medical record information, or other PHI. DFEAL's assessment questionnaires are specifically designed to collect aggregate organisational data — not patient-level records.
